Before the storm arrives

  • Charge phones and backup batteries
  • Fill water jugs (and the bathtub)
  • Set out flashlights — not candles
  • 7-day supply of medications
  • Fuel the car; cash on hand
  • Generator: check fuel, test run it
  • Secure or haul boats; double lines
  • Bring in outdoor furniture
  • Check on a neighbor — agree on a signal

During an outage

  • Keep fridge and freezer doors closed
  • Never run a generator indoors or in the garage
  • Stay far from any downed line — call 911
  • Check on your neighbor again
